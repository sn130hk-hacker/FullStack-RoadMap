## üí° Tips for Sticking to Schedule

1. **Set Realistic Goals:** Don't overcommit
2. **Track Your Time:** Use time tracking apps
3. **Take Breaks:** Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
4. **Stay Consistent:** Code every day, even if just 30 minutes
5. **Join Study Groups:** Accountability helps
6. **Celebrate Milestones:** Reward yourself for achievements
7. **Adjust as Needed:** Life happens, be flexible
8. **Focus on Quality:** Better to learn deeply than rush
